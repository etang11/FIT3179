Setting the Scene

This website is aimed at providing snippets of visualisations through a select few idioms in relation to airbnb's in New York City in the year 2019. Please note this is the 2nd assignment for the unit FIT3179 - Data Visualisation, where the tools utilised in creating these visualisation relied predomninately on vega-lite and basic html and css.

Dispersion of properties in New York City

The dot map on the right aims to illustrate the dispersion or distribution of airbnb properties throughout the city of New York. The attribute of price has also been encoded through colour saturation the selected scale.

From what it illustrates is that there are not many properties in Staten Island when compared to other Boroughs. Which could be indicative of the popular attractions in or around the boroughs of Queens, Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens and the Bronx. The heaviest distribution can be seen in the borough Manhattan - whereby the price per night is also seen to be concentrated in that area. The big attraction point in this area could be explained with the Central Park which is seen with the rectangular shape in Manhattan.
